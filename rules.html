Oliom Horizons Rules

These are the general community rules for worlds, profiles, comments, friend requests, private chats, mini chats, and online game sessions.

1. Create original workDo not copy, re-upload, steal, or slightly rename someone else’s project, world, logo, map, scripts, or assets.
2. Respect other creatorsNo harassment, threats, impersonation, humiliation, or drama targeting other users.
3. No politics or culture-war contentOliom Horizons is for building and playing, not political arguments.
4. No religious debates or targetingDo not attack, mock, pressure, or debate people based on religion.
5. No orientation or identity argumentsDo not target, insult, debate, or provoke people because of orientation, identity, or personal traits.
6. No datingOliom Horizons is not a dating platform.
7. Keep content appropriateNo sexual content, extreme violence, hate symbols, scams, malicious links, or shock content.
8. Safe scripts onlyDo not create scripts intended to crash, exploit, steal data, or break other worlds.
