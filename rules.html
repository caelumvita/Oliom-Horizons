Oliom Horizons Rules

These are the general community rules for worlds, profiles, comments, and future online servers.

1. Create original workDo not copy, re-upload, steal, or slightly rename someone else’s project, world, logo, map, scripts, or assets. Inspiration is okay; direct copying is not.
2. Respect other creatorsNo harassment, threats, impersonation, humiliation, or drama targeting other users. Criticism should be calm and useful.
3. No politics or culture-war contentOliom Horizons is for building and playing. Do not use worlds, profiles, comments, or servers to promote political arguments, campaigns, or controversial real-world disputes.
4. No religious debates or targetingDo not attack, mock, pressure, or debate people based on religion. Keep the platform neutral and friendly for everyone.
5. No orientation or identity argumentsDo not target, insult, debate, or provoke people because of orientation, identity, or personal traits. Everyone should be able to build safely.
6. No dating or romantic roleplayOliom Horizons is not a dating platform. Do not use profiles, servers, comments, or games for dating, romantic roleplay, flirting, or relationship-seeking.
7. Keep content appropriateNo sexual content, extreme violence, hate symbols, scams, malicious links, or anything designed to shock or harm users.
8. Safe scripts onlyOHS scripts and future uploaded worlds should be understandable and safe. Do not create scripts intended to crash, exploit, steal data, or break other worlds.
9. No spamDo not flood comments, servers, names, or profiles with repeated text, fake servers, advertisements, or misleading content.
10. Moderation can remove contentWhen online features are added, unsafe or rule-breaking worlds, comments, profiles, or servers may be hidden or removed.
